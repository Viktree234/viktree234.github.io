Yoruba History Exam Prep

History & Origin

Origin: The Yoruba trace their ancestral origin to Ile-Ife.

Founding Ancestor: Odùduwà, sent by Olódùmarè.

Kingdoms: Oyo Empire, Ijebu, Ekiti, and others.

Art: Famous for bronze and terracotta works.

Global Influence: Spread to the Americas via the transatlantic slave trade, influencing Santería, Candomblé, and Vodun.
